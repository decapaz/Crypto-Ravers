The Specs

A 64x64 pixel canvas has been used for the creation of the NFT's. Crypto Ravers NFT's metadata are stored on Arweave, a permanent decentralized data storage. Each Raver is generated from over 56 possible traits spread over 6 layers, some being rarer than others, giving everyone the chance to mint the rarest ones! Let's see who's lucky! With a minting cost of as low as 0.5 SOL, we are giving the opportunity to everyone to get in the hype. Gas fees are being extremely low, and the cost is always the same for everybody.

  • 56 different traits.
